Our Trips

On this route you can challenge the "devil" himself as you pass through the famous "devil's teeth" river. It bears this name because of local urban myths that say that the devil himself dared to pass through this river and as he passed he dropped a tooth as a mark of his territory.

The Titicaca River runs through the fastest waters in the entire country; thousands of people describe it as the most aggressive and wildest of all rivers. One of the favorite routes of our clientele

Los Rapidos del Tunari, located in the city of Cochabamba. A tourist site full of energy. It is located west of the city and it takes 2 hours to reach the river. The views are wonderful, a luxury to take photographs along the way and on the way down we pass through a local town.

Located in the north of Bolivia, in the city of Beni. One of the largest rivers, in addition to the hot waters that circulate in it. An incredible opportunity to take photographs of the Beni wildlife, since along the way you can see animals on the sides (Which are protected by meshes)
