We started in 2011, with 10 members and all the will to increase our passion for rafting to our family. That will to increase was bigger and bigger and we wanted everyone to be part of this passion. Wave Zone Rafting has all what you are looking for: Adrenaline, adventure, safety and a lot of fun!. We want you to be part of our family and everyday become a better place for all. We are the perfect way to forget the problems of the life and connect with the nature, in the same way we connect to ourselfs.

Our guides are a passionate people for the rafting, personal capable to do this sport. Our routes are the best in our country. As we get an adventure we can take photos of this moments, we learn more about our skills. Before we start the route, we train a little without the boat and we learn our names (If we are not known) to be part of a team and help everybody to have a good experience. We learn together how to work as a team.
